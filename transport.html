Getting Around Tokyo

IC/Pasmo Cards

Pasmo and Suica are accepted on the Tokyo Metro, as well as on railway stations. Transfers between Tokyo Metro subway lines and Toei Subway lines are usually not free.

JR East Railways

Railway lines of JR East primarily serve the Kanto and Tohoku regions. Tokyo is located in the Kanto Region and many local rails run inside of Tokyo, offering more coverage.

shinkansen

The shinkansen is the number one mode of transportation for visiting places outside of Tokyo. Its fast speed and coverage of Japan makes it perfect for day trips.

Buses

Bus lines are a convenient method of transportation, especiually to newer areas where the metro does not cover such as Odaiba, and the wards along the outskirts of Tokyo.
